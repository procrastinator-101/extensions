Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Asura Manager ("we", "our", or "the extension") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection

Asura Manager does not collect any personal information from users. All data is stored locally on your device.

  • Reading progress, chapter history, and series information are saved locally in chrome.storage.local.
  • No usernames, passwords, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information are collected or transmitted.

2. How Data is Used

All data stored by the extension is used solely to:

  • Track your reading progress
  • Display your progress dashboard within the extension popup

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

4. Clearing Data

You can clear all saved progress at any time using the "Clear All" button in the extension popup. Once cleared, this data cannot be recovered.

5. Security

Data is stored locally in your browser's storage. While we take steps to ensure the extension does not access external sites, your device and Chrome browser's security practices also help protect this data.

6. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be included in the extension and updated on the Chrome Web Store.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact the extension developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.

Enjoy reading safely and privately with Asura Manager!
